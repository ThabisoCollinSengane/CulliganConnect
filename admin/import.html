Import & assign cases

In Salesforce, click into the case list, press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy every row, then paste below. CulliganConnect reads the columns — Case Number, Account Name, Subject, Status, Date/Time Opened, Case Type — and shares the cases out to your agents. Pick “By case type” to auto-route each case to its department (Water Ordering, Service Request, Account Update, Tech Support).
